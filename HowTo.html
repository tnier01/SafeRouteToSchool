Explanation of statistics

All data were taken from the German Accident Atlas. The shown data is from 2019.
FAQs and explanations of accident participants and the heaviest accident result regarding injuries can be found here: German Accident Atlas | explanatory notes
Data of previous years can be found here: German Accident Atlas | OpenData. As well as additional information within the downloadable PDF containing the Metadata.

Risk level explanation

The risk levels were derived from the accidents. Therefore a density map with 5 levels was created. That means, level 1 has the lowest density of accidents and level 5 the highest density. With this level 5 routes avoid areas where the most accidents happened, while level 3 routes already avoid areas with fewer accidents. The possibility to avoid level 1 or 2 is not given, because with this you could drive almost nowhere.
